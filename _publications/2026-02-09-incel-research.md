**DOI:** [10.1080/17539153.2026.2629061](https://doi.org/10.1080/17539153.2026.2629061)

### Abstract
This research features a semi-structured interview conducted with a self-identified former Incel who participated in the subculture from 1997 to the mid-2010s. It examines an insider, lived perspective on where current Incel research may mislead or overlook key details, and provides recommendations for improvement.
